Frequently Asked Roadmaps

Fatigue is often about the chores; a need to move is about the identity. If your home no longer feels like a sanctuary that protects your peace, it has likely become a "storage unit" for a version of yourself you've already outgrown.
The tax is paid in time. Waiting 18 months for a 1% drop might save you money on paper, but it costs you 500+ days of living in a space that is shrinking your potential. You can refinance a rate, but you can't "refinance" lost years.
If the location is perfect but the layout is wrong, renovate. If the location is the "ceiling" on your lifestyle, no amount of granite countertops will fix that. Move.
Proximity is the most undervalued asset. Living where your peers and mentors live creates organic networking at the gym and the cafe. It’s a career accelerator.
Regret usually stems from compromise. People compromise on the neighborhood to get the extra bedroom, then realize they hate the drive. Our roadmap prevents those trade-offs.
